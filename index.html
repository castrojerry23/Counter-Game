RWBY Counter

At the begining of the game, Ruby, Weiss, Blake and Yang are assigned a certain point ammount. When they are clicked, whatever ammount they are assigned will be submitted to your number ammount that you are trying to match to the randomly generated computer's number. If you match the computer's guess correctly, you will win the game. If you go over the number that the computer chooses, you will lose the game. The point ammount for team RWBY will also randomly generate with each new game started!
